About Us

We are a group of passionate architecture majors at NJIT, committed to shaping the built environment with creativity, precision, and sustainability. Our program focuses on blending technical skills with artistic design, preparing us to solve complex problems in modern architecture.

At NJIT, we are inspired by the latest trends in architecture, working with cutting-edge tools and learning from industry experts. Together, we aspire to contribute to the future of architecture, creating spaces that inspire and serve communities around the world.
